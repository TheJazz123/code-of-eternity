
- 'go [direction]': Move in a direction (north, south, east, west)
- 'look': Observe your surroundings
- 'look at [item]': Inspect a specific item in detail
- 'take [item]': Take an item and add it to your inventory
- 'use': Use an item in your inventory.
- 'inventory': View the items you're carrying.
- 'map': Display the minimap of Scriptoria
- 'exit': Quit the game
You'll learn more commands as you progress, but these basics will get you started.
